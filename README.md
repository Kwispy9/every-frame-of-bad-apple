# Every Frame of Bad Apple
This is just a repository for anyone who needs every frame of Bad Apple for creating their own version of this meme. You may use these resources however you like, as I am not the original creator of this music video, and I wouldn't care anyways.
# How to Download
If you're here to download all frames of Bad Apple to make your own, I doubt you wouldn't know how to download all of these files. But, on the off-chance that you don't it's really simple. Just navigate over to the green button labeled "Code," click on the drop-down and select "Download ZIP." There, you got it, congrats.
